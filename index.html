About Me

I'm a doctoral researcher in sociology at médialab Sciences Po Paris. I work under the supervision of Sylvain Parasie and Pedro Ramaciotti Morales. My research areas focus on processes linked to media and politics in the digital age in France. I use a lot of quantitative methods, especially Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Econometrics.

On this website, I will share my academic and personal works.
